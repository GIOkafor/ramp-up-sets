How to Use the App

  1. Enter Your Working Weight: Input the weight you plan to lift.
  2. Enter Reps: Specify how many reps you will perform for your working set.
  3. Select Weight Unit: Use the toggle to choose between kilograms (kg) and pounds (lbs).
  4. Follow the Warm-Up Plan: Perform the listed sets to prepare for your workout.

How It Works

This app helps you calculate the correct weights to use for warm-up sets, ensuring you maximize strength output while reducing the risk of injury.

By gradually increasing the load, you prepare your muscles and nervous system for your working set, improving performance and efficiency in the gym.

Benefits of Ramp-Up Sets

  • Maximize Strength Output: Proper warm-ups prepare your body for heavier lifts, enhancing performance.
  • Prevent Injury: Gradual loading reduces strain on joints and muscles.
  • Improve Muscle Activation: Activates the right muscles for optimal movement and power.
  • Save Time: Efficient warm-ups get you ready faster without unnecessary fatigue.
